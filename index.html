Chillax is an RSS reader that lets you kick back and read a bit.

To the far left are some feeds; click the plus button to add your own.

Use your scrollwheel/trackpad to scroll through the list of news items.

You can read articles you've already downloaded if you go offline.

Everything's stored within your browser using Web SQL.
